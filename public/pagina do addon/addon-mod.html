Lethal Craft

By JuliusScizzor

Category: Horror

23K Downloads

An interesting horror map for you...

This is a Minecraft Bedrock map for v1.21.30+ LethalCraft brings the terrifyingly funny experience of the hit co-op survival horror game Lethal Company by Zeekerss into Minecraft. The goal was to try to re-create the actual game as close as we could in the bounds of Minecraft Bedrock addons. This map features Proximity Chat on Minecraft Bedrock Edition, using SineVector’s VoiceCraft.
